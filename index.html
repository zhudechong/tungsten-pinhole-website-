About PicomTech

At PicomTech, we specialize in manufacturing high-precision tungsten pinholes designed for synchrotron radiation imaging applications. Our pinholes offer exceptional durability and aperture control, making them ideal for beamline measurements, imaging, and diagnostics at synchrotron facilities.

Key Specifications

Aperture Range 10 µm – 100 µm
Thickness 4 mm
Aperture Accuracy ±3 µm
Material Tungsten
Applications Synchrotron radiation imaging, X-ray beam diagnostics
