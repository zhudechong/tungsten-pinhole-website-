Product Overview

At PicomTech, we specialize in manufacturing high-precision tungsten pinholes for synchrotron radiation applications. Our pinholes are fabricated by precision machining tungsten into stepped slit components, which are then assembled in horizontal and vertical directions to form a pinhole structure. This design is intended for X-ray pinhole camera systems at beam diagnostic beamlines, primarily for measuring transverse beam sizes and emittance in storage rings.

Key Specifications

Specification Details
Aperture Range 10 μm – 100 μm
Thickness 4 mm
Aperture Accuracy ±3 μm
