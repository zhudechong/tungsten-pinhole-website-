High Precision Tungsten Pinhole

For Synchrotron Radiation Pinhole Imaging

Product Overview

This high precision tungsten pinhole is designed for synchrotron radiation applications, offering superior aperture control and durability. The pinhole is made of high-quality tungsten and is ideal for X-ray beamline measurements, imaging, and diagnostics.

This high-precision tungsten pinhole is used in X-ray pinhole cameras for beam size and emittance measurement in synchrotron storage rings, providing essential diagnostics for beam quality and performance assessment.

Key Specifications

Specification Details
